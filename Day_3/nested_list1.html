Daily Meal Plan

  1. Breakfast

    • Main Dish: Pancakes
      • Maple Syrup
      • Fresh Barries
      • Whipped Cream
    • Side: Fruit Salad
    • Drink: Orange Juice

  2. Lunch

    1. Main Course: Build Your Own Sandwich
      1. Start with two slices of Sourdough Bread
      2. Add a layer of Turkey slices.
      3. Place Swiss Cheese on top.
      4. Finish with Lettus and Toamato.
    2. Side: Potato Chips
    3. Beverage: Iced Tea

  3. Dinner

    • Appetizer
    • Main Course: Spaghetti Balognese
      1. First, place the cooked Spaghetti.
      2. Next, ladle the golognese sauce over the pasta.
      3. Finally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan chese.
    • Dessert: Tiramisu
